### How It Works

1. **Click the extension icon** - Your topic cards appear instantly
2. **Quick access** - Click any link box on a card to open that website
3. **View all links** - Click the topic card or "More" button to see all links in that topic
4. **Bulk open** - Click "Select URLs" to choose multiple links and open them together in a color-coded tab group
5. **Customize** - Click the gear icon to add topics, change themes, or manage links
