# Privacy Policy for Pandora Box

**Last Updated:** January 26, 2026

## Overview

Pandora Box is a Chrome extension that provides quick access to categorized webpage shortcuts. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

### Local Storage Only

Pandora Box stores the following information **locally on your device** using Chrome's storage API:

- **Topic Categories**: Your custom topic names, icons, and colors
- **URL Bookmarks**: The list of URLs you've added to each topic
- **User Preferences**: Settings such as theme preferences and topic order

**Important**: All data is stored locally in your browser. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Pandora Box does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Browsing history
- Search queries
- Analytics or usage statistics
- IP addresses
- Cookies or tracking data
- Any information about the websites you visit

## How We Use Your Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

- Display your customized topic categories and URLs
- Remember your preferences between browser sessions
- Organize tabs into colored tab groups based on your topics

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:

- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your data
- Use your data for advertising
- Send your data to external servers
- Include any analytics or tracking services

## Permissions Explained

Pandora Box requests the following Chrome permissions:

- **tabs**: To query the current tab when using the "pin current page" feature, allowing you to quickly add the page you're viewing to a topic
- **tabGroups**: To create and organize browser tabs into colored groups when opening multiple links
- **storage**: To save your topics and preferences locally on your device

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- Your data remains under your control
- No network transmission of your personal information occurs
- Data is protected by your browser's security measures

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: View all your topics and URLs within the extension
- **Modify**: Edit or update any topics or URLs at any time
- **Delete**: Remove individual URLs, entire topics, or uninstall the extension to delete all data

## Third-Party Websites

When you click on a URL in Pandora Box, you are directed to external websites. This privacy policy does not apply to those third-party websites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

## Children's Privacy

Pandora Box does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The extension is designed for general use and does not require any personal information.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
